title: Custom Domain
description: Route Kuest services through subdomains you control.

---

Use a reverse proxy to keep Kuest service URLs under your domain.

<Steps titleSize="h3">
  <Step title="Create the service proxies">
    <Tabs sync={false}>
      <Tab title="Deploy to Cloudflare" icon="cloud">
        Recommended. Enter your domain when Cloudflare asks for `DOMAIN`; the deployment creates the Worker, DNS records, and certificates.

        <a href="https://deploy.workers.cloudflare.com/?url=https://github.com/kuestcom/custom-domain">
          <img src="https://deploy.workers.cloudflare.com/button" alt="Deploy to Cloudflare" noZoom />
        </a>

        Use the root domain without `https://`, such as `example.com`.
      </Tab>

      <Tab title="Manual setup" icon="settings">
        <AccordionGroup>
          <Accordion title="Cloudflare dashboard">
            Create a Worker with a `DOMAIN` variable, then attach these hostnames under **Settings > Domains & Routes > Add > Custom Domain**:

            ```text
            relayer.yourdomain.com
            clob.yourdomain.com
            data-api.yourdomain.com
            user-pnl-api.yourdomain.com
            community.yourdomain.com
            create-market.yourdomain.com
            gamma-api.yourdomain.com
            price-reference.yourdomain.com
            geoblock.yourdomain.com
            sdk-download.yourdomain.com
            ws-live-data.yourdomain.com
            ws-subscriptions-clob.yourdomain.com
            ```

            Paste the [dashboard Worker](https://github.com/kuestcom/custom-domain/blob/main/examples/dashboard-worker.js) into the Cloudflare editor.
          </Accordion>

          <Accordion title="Other providers">
            Create the same 12 subdomains as reverse proxies. Each hostname must forward its path, query, headers, body, and WebSocket upgrades to the matching `*.kuest.com` hostname listed in [`services.json`](https://github.com/kuestcom/custom-domain/blob/main/services.json).
          </Accordion>
        </AccordionGroup>
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify the proxy">
    Open `https://clob.yourdomain.com` and confirm it reaches the Kuest CLOB service.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Update environment variables">
    In Vercel or your hosting provider, replace `yourdomain.com` and save:

    ```bash
    CREATE_MARKET_URL=https://create-market.yourdomain.com
    DATA_URL=https://data-api.yourdomain.com
    CLOB_URL=https://clob.yourdomain.com
    RELAYER_URL=https://relayer.yourdomain.com
    USER_PNL_URL=https://user-pnl-api.yourdomain.com
    GAMMA_URL=https://gamma-api.yourdomain.com
    COMMUNITY_URL=https://community.yourdomain.com
    PRICE_REFERENCE_URL=https://price-reference.yourdomain.com
    GEOBLOCK_URL=https://geoblock.yourdomain.com
    SDK_DOWNLOAD_URL=https://sdk-download.yourdomain.com
    WS_LIVE_DATA_URL=wss://ws-live-data.yourdomain.com
    WS_CLOB_URL=wss://ws-subscriptions-clob.yourdomain.com
    ```

    Redeploy the application after saving the variables.
  </Step>
</Steps>
